FAQ
Questions? Answered.
Is SkolMatrixa suitable for small institutions? ›
Absolutely. SkolMatrixa scales from a 50-student coaching centre to a school with 3,000+ students. Our free tier is perfect for getting started.
How long does the initial setup take? ›
Most institutions complete full setup in under 30 minutes using our guided wizard. Adding students and staff can be done individually or via CSV bulk import.
Is my institution's data secure? ›
Yes. Every institution's data is completely isolated in our multi-tenant architecture. We use enterprise-grade encryption, automated daily backups, and maintain 99.9% uptime.
Can students and parents access the platform? ›
Yes. SkolMatrixa includes a dedicated student portal to view assignments, grades, attendance, and fee status. Parents receive automated email notifications.
Do you offer a free trial? ›
Yes. Our Starter plan is completely free for up to 100 students. You can upgrade to Professional or Enterprise at any time as your institution grows.
What makes SkolMatrixa different? ›
SkolMatrixa is purpose-built for Indian schools and coaching institutes with multi-tenant isolation, role-based access, and comprehensive management features in one integrated platform.
